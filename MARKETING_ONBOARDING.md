## 1. What Is Afferens?

An API that gives AI agents real-time physical senses — vision, GPS, temperature, sound, air quality, hardware health. Developers connect their agents with one line of code and get back structured sensory data.

**Free tier: 10,000 tokens, no card required.**
**Live at:** https://afferens.com

Your job is to make the right developers find Afferens, understand it in seconds, and sign up.

## 2. Step 0 — Use the Product First

Before you post anything about Afferens, use it.

1. Sign up at **afferens.com/signup**

## 3. The Three ICPs (Who We're Talking To)

Every piece of content should be written for one of these people:

| ICP | Who they are | What they care about |
|-----|-------------|---------------------|
| Solo Hacker / YC Applicant | Indie dev, 1-5 people, building AI agents or robotics | Does it work? Free tier? Fast integration? |
| AI Agent Startup | Seed/Series A, 5-50 people, agent-first product | Reliability, scale, integration effort |
| Enterprise (Robotics/Logistics) | 500+ employees, physical operations | Security, compliance, vendor stability |

Content primarily targets the first two. Enterprise comes later.

## 4. Core Positioning

**One-line anchor:** "Senses for AI agents."

Everything else expands from that. The problem: AI agents are blind and deaf — they can't perceive the physical world. The solution: Afferens gives them 6 sensory modalities via a single REST API.

**What NOT to say:**
- No fabricated metrics. If we don't have the number, don't use it.
- No fabricated case studies. We have no case studies yet.
- No Python SDK — the API is REST/curl only.
- No "we" in content — write declarative statements about the product.
- No emojis in technical posts (Reddit, HN, LinkedIn).

Tone: direct, technical, no fluff. You are talking to people who will immediately test a claim.
